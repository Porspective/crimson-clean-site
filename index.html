ABOUT US

We’re a student-run pressure washing company based in Norman, OK. Our mission is to deliver exceptional results while raising funds for our education and donating to children’s health causes.

At Crimson Clean Co., we believe in hard work, honesty, and giving back to the community. Our team of University of Oklahoma students is dedicated to delivering high-quality service to both residential and commercial clients.

We specialize in cleaning driveways, trash bins, decks, siding, and more, using eco-friendly methods and professional-grade equipment. We treat every job like it’s our own home and go the extra mile to ensure customer satisfaction.

Supporting Crimson Clean Co. means supporting local students' dreams and making a real impact on the community. We’re not just washing away dirt — we’re building futures.
